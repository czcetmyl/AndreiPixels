About

Every frame tells a story

Andrei Pixels is a passionate wedding and event photographer dedicated to capturing the raw, authentic emotions of your most precious moments. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling through the lens, every photo is a masterpiece.

From the nervous excitement of preparation to the joyful chaos of the reception — every fleeting second is worth preserving. We don't just take photos; we craft visual memories you'll treasure for a lifetime.
